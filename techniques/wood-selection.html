Wood Selection Tips for Smoking

Essential Wood Types

Mild Woods

  • Apple - Sweet and fruity
  • Cherry - Mild and sweet
  • Maple - Light and sweet
  • Peach - Subtle and fruity

Strong Woods

  • Hickory - Bold and savory
  • Mesquite - Intense and earthy
  • Oak - Strong and traditional
  • Walnut - Rich and heavy

Best Meat Pairings

Wood Type Best For Smoke Intensity
Apple Pork, Poultry, Fish Mild
Hickory Ribs, Brisket, Pork Shoulder Strong
Cherry Poultry, Ham, Fish Mild
