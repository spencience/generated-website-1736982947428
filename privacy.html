Privacy Policy

Data Collection & Usage

At Mount Spencer, we prioritize the protection of your digital footprints as carefully as we maintain our physical trails. We collect minimal data necessary for your virtual climbing experience:

  • Summit log entries and achievements
  • Virtual climbing progress and preferences
  • Weather alert subscriptions
  • Trail condition reports

Data Security

Your data is protected with enterprise-grade security measures, including:

  • End-to-end encryption for virtual climbing sessions
  • Secure storage of personal achievements
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Optional two-factor authentication for summit logs

Your Rights

You maintain control over your data with the ability to:

  • Download your climbing history and achievements
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Update your privacy preferences
