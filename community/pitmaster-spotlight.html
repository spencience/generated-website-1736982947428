Pitmaster of the Month: Ray "Smoke Ring" Johnson

Meet Ray Johnson

With over 30 years of experience smoking meats, Ray "Smoke Ring" Johnson has become a legend in the Texas BBQ scene. His signature brisket technique has won him countless awards and earned him a devoted following at his Austin-based restaurant, "Ring of Fire BBQ."
